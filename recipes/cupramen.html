Description

A timeless staple for a quick and easy meal

Ingredients

  • Your choice of instant Cup Ramen
  • Hot water
  • Any preferred additions, such as extra meat, vegetables, etc.

Steps

  1. Open cup ramen halfway.
  2. Boil water and add to fill line on ramen cup.
  3. Replace lid and keep covered for 3 to 5 minutes.
  4. Prepare and add preferred extra ingredients after noodles in cup are tender.
  5. Enjoy!
