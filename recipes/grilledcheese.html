Grilled Cheese

Description

Delicious toasted sandwich that pairs well with tomato soup.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices bread
  • 2 slices cheese
  • 2 tsp butter

Steps

  1. Melt 1 tsp butter in a frying pan on medium heat.
  2. Place 2 slices cheese in between 2 slices of bread.
  3. Place sandwich in pan and let bread absorb melted butter. Toast until desired color, then remove.
  4. Add last 1 tsp butter to pan and melt.
  5. Add sandwich non-toasted side down and toast until desired color.
  6. Remove and enjoy!
