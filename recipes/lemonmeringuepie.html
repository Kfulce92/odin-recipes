Lemon Meringue Pie

Description

This lemon meringue pie is a southern classic! Made almost entirely from scratch and tastes just like old times at Grandma's house.

Ingredients

  • Graham Cracker Crust
  • 1/3 C Lemon Juice
  • 2 eggs, seperated yolk from white
  • 1 can of Condensed Milk
  • 2 lemons
  • 1/4 C Sugar

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees Farenheit.
  2. Grate lemon peel from lemons
  3. Combine lemon peel, lemon juice, egg yolks, and condensed milk in a bowl.
  4. Place mixture in pie crust.
  5. Whip egg whites until foamy, then add sugar. Continue to whip until stiff peaks form.
  6. Place whipped egg whites on top of pie, using a spoon to form small peaks if desired.
  7. Bake pie until merengue is golden brown on peaks
  8. Allow pie to cool completely, store in refrigerator after completely cooled.
